ECE Summer Institute (cross-listed with ECED 480G / 952). Special Course in Early Childhood Education: Supporting Mental Health and Wellness in Early Childhood Education [Working Title]. Synchronous/Asynchronous.

Runs Monday to Friday, Jul 4 to 8, 2022.

This course explores contemporary factors for supporting the mental health and wellness of young children and discusses the relationship between mental health and wellness and early childhood education for positive social-emotional development and quality of experience. There will be a special focus on family-centred approaches to mental health and wellness and support for children with neurodevelopmental differences.

Note: ECED graduate students register in ECED 565A / 952.

1) Initial restriction: DEDU; CEYED; BHK; BKIN.

2) Restriction will be removed for UNCL on March 10, 2022.

3) Restriction will be removed for all undergraduate students on March 14, 2022.

A waitlist is available for this course. Inquiries: eced.educ@ubc.ca.
